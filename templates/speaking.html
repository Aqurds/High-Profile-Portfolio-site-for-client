We are honoured to welcome Jacobs Edo as a keynote speaker during a class for master students at the IMC University of Applied Sciences Krems on Saturday March 23 at 9 am. Jacobs is CSO at OFID, a digital transformation expert and author of the book: Digital Transformation: Evolving a digitally enabled Nigerian Public Service. The topic of the keynote will be "Digital Transformation in the Public Sector" followed by a discussion with students of the master degree DIGITAL BUSINESS INNOVATION AND TRANSFORMATION.

Digitisation has existed since the invention of computer systems with its current iteration, digitisation and digital transformation are both epoch-defining phenomenon for countries, organisations and people. Developments in digital technologies are unprecedented in their velocity, scale and impact, transforming products, services, operations and lifestyles.

The advent of the digital economy – marked by the emergence of “digital disruptors” as direct threats to established market leaders and prevailing economic understandings – poses a clear and present danger to legacy businesses: transform or die. The emerging digital economy has the potential to generate new scientific research and breakthroughs, fuelling job opportunities, economic growth, and improving how we live our lives.

This is the fourth industrial revolution and it’s going to have a significant impact on the global economy as well. For me, the digital economy represents a renaissance of sorts, an opportunity to take a frontline leadership role in transforming businesses and becoming a standard bearer for driving corporate performance through digital transformation. Remember change is the only constant in the world.

Jacobs is the go-to-expert on enterprise digital transformation having participated in numerous reform projects globally. He has nearly two decades of research knowledge and practical hands-on implementation experience, ranging from system architecture and artificial intelligence to creative business processes and operations, to building a culture of leadership in innovation. With deep insights, system expertise, a captivating personality, and a high level of exposure – having spoken globally, Jake will deliver excellent value at your next event.
