20+ YEARS

Senior Information Technology Professional

Jacobs Edo is the author of the best-selling book “Digital Transformation: Evolving a digitally enabled Nigerian Public Service”, co-chair: United Nations SAP Special Interest Group (UNSIG), co-founder of the Austrian based DigTrans – A Think Tank Group for Digital Transformation and the chair of the Vienna Digital Transformation Meetup.

Jacobs is a globally certified enterprise architect, trusted advisor, and a public speaker. He is known for his dedication and tireless efforts in ethics, change management and digital transformation. He earned a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electrical Engineering (Second Class Upper Division) from the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, a diploma in Information Technology from Robert Gordon University, Aberdeen, an MSc degree in Telecommunication and Internet Technologies from the Technical University of Applied Sciences, Vienna and a Global Business Transformation Management (GBTM) Master’s certification by SAP AG and the University of Applied Sciences and Arts, Northwestern Switzerland’s Business Transformation Academy.

As a volunteer Consultant for the Stanford Institute for Innovation in Developing Economies (Stanford SEED), he supports its vision of bringing the power of digital innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership to established businesses in sub-Saharan Africa and their leaders on the ground and their communities. He is currently writing his doctoral dissertation at the Colorado Technical University, the USA on Big Data Analytics.

Jacobs is the go-to-expert on enterprise digital transformation having participated in numerous reform projects globally. He has nearly two decades of research knowledge and practical hands-on implementation experience, ranging from system architecture and artificial intelligence to creative business processes and operations, to building a culture of leadership in innovation. With profound insights, system expertise, a captivating personality, and high level of exposure – having spoken globally, Jake will deliver excellent values to your organisation.
